About

Emma has over 25 years of experience in the fitness industry, working in studios and gyms both in England and abroad. She was introduced to Pilates early in her career and after being convinced of the benefits of this method, chose to train with both Modern Pilates and The Pilates Foundation. Since qualifying she has spent the past 15 years working with clients of all ages and levels of fitness. Her classes offer a wide range of levels and options, therefore ensuring everyone can work at a pace where they feel challenged but comfortable.

Created by Joseph Pilates in the 1920's, Pilates is a form of exercise which focuses on core strength, posture, balance and flexibility. Because each exercise can be modified to provide either a gentle strength training program or, a more challenging one, it can be suitable for both beginners and, people who exercise regularly.
